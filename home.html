My Home

Debut EP

"My Home" by Myles Smith conveys the profound love and emotional bond between two individuals, expressing the idea that true home is not merely a physical place, but rather the presence of a loved one who brings comfort, warmth, and a sense of belonging.

Stargazing

Debut EP

Smith got the inspiration for "Stargazing" while hanging out in LA. "I love the idea that the person we fall in love with has been there our whole life," he said.

Wait For You

Debut EP

"Wait For You" is a song about the kind of love that packs an emotional thermos and prepares to stick around for the long haul. It's a tender ballad where Myles Smith sings of the tension of sticking by someone, no matter what. Even when you're miles apart, you're still there, waiting for them.
